Amazon Product Reviews Sentiment Analysis


CONCEPT

This project is my opportunity to delve into the fascinating realm of sentiment analysis, using the natural language toolkit (nltk) machine learning library. Armed with a dataset of Amazon product reviews, I'll unravel the secrets of consumer sentiment and provide online shoppers with insights that will leave them spellbound. Get ready to embark on this interactive retail data adventure, where I'll craft shopping insights and unlock the true potential of these captivating reviews.
In this world of data-driven retail, I am the curator, the data wizard, and the storyteller. My dedication to cleaning, analyzing, and implementing sentiment analysis adds to the magic of online shopping, making every purchase extraordinary. My journey is one of data, commerce, and endless possibilities.

TOOLS USED

Tools used in this project:

  • Python
  • NLTK
  • ML
  • Tableau
